Our Services

Pressure Washing

Decks, driveways, fenceing, railings, patios, roofs, home sidind concrete, and anything else that you can think of outside and around your home that could use a deep cleaning.

Wallpaper

Sometimes paint is just not enough. Wallpaper is a classic option to add a new dimension of texture and pattern to a wall or room.

Drywall Repairs

That's right, we don't just paint walls, we can also fix them! If you need some holes or dents repaired in the drywall we can take care of those and cover it with a layer of paint.

Staining

Cabinets, fencing, decks, railings, and anything that is wood that needs a stain.

Minor Repairs And Repaint

If you have any minor repairs needed on your wall or trim, let us know! We can fix them for you before we paint so you wall can look like new.

Kitchen Cabinets

Color in the kitchen can completely change the look of the room. One of the best ways is to not only add a new color to the walls, but also the cabinets!

Interior/Exterior

Whether it's inside or out, we have the equipment and experience to get the job done.

Millwork Painting

Millwork is often called woodwork. These include baseboards, crown molding, mantels, window and door frames, chairs, paneling, or stair rails. It can be stained or painted or even finished with a clear seal.

Accent Walls

A little pop of color goes a long way. Sometimes you don't have to paint all the walls of a room, just one or two. It's a great way to add variety without going overboard.
